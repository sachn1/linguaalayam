Search options
Quick Finds words with similar spelling. Works well for approximate matches and typos — runing finds running.
Exact Looks up the word exactly as typed (case-insensitive). Use when you know the precise spelling.
Smart Finds words by meaning — type a description or phrase: "complete absence of sound", "feeling of nostalgia", "to move quickly on foot". No LLM key required for search. Add an API key to get a prose explanation instead of raw entries.
English → Malayalam Translate an English word to Malayalam. Uses the Olam dictionary.
Thesaurus Find English synonyms and their Malayalam equivalents. Uses the Ekkurup thesaurus.
Malayalam dictionary Look up a Malayalam word with its Malayalam definition. Uses the Datuk dictionary.
